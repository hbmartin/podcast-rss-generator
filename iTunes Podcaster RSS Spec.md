# A Podcaster’s Guide to RSS

An [RSS feed](https://help.apple.com/itc/podcasts_connect/#/itcadbcdbe3d) contains all the [metadata](https://help.apple.com/itc/podcasts_connect/#/itc7b5b1998e) of a podcast. This information governs what listeners will see about your podcast on Apple’s services: from the the show art, to whether a podcast shows up in relevant searches, to episode titles and descriptions.

All metadata in an RSS feed is stored within element tags (here is a [RSS Feed Sample](https://help.apple.com/itc/podcasts_connect/#/itcbaf351599) for reference). Each tag is applicable to either [show](https://help.apple.com/itc/podcasts_connect/#/itca5dea085b) data (contained within the `<channel>` tag) or [episode](https://help.apple.com/itc/podcasts_connect/#/itca322a0a75) data (contained within an `<item>` tag). This guide will explain what data to include and how it should be structured.

The figure below depicts a “Show view” from the Apple Podcasts app and labels how `<item>` tags map to episode information. It’s important to note that the `<itunes:type>` show tag listed above affects how episodes are displayed. Please note: Apple will display the latest 2,000 episodes. Of these, only valid episodes are displayed on the show page.

By default shows are categorized as *episodic* meaning the most recent episodes will show up first and the `<pubDate>` of an episode will be displayed. If you specify your show `<itunes:type>` to be *serial*, which requires also specifying `<itunes:episode>` numbers for each episode, then episodes will be listed in sequential order.

Apple Podcasts accepts [RSS 2.0](http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/rss/rss.html) tags as well as tags specific to Apple Podcasts and the Podcast namespace.

When you use tags specific to Apple Podcasts or the Podcast namespace, indicated by a leading `<itunes>` or `<podcast>` prefix, you must add the following namespace declaration as the second line in your XML:

```
<rss xmlns:itunes="http://www.itunes.com/dtds/podcast-1.0.dtd" xmlns:podcast="https://podcastindex.org/namespace/1.0" version="2.0">
```

Without this declaration, Apple Podcasts ignores all tags specific to Apple Podcasts or the Podcast namespace. The declaration is case sensitive, and must be entered as shown. Other declarations may also exist in the same tag.

Use plain text UTF-8 encoding for your feed. Tag values are limited to 255 characters unless otherwise specified above. Don't add leading or trailing spaces to your values.
